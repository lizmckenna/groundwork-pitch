Core Feature

Voice-First 1-on-1 Notes

Design target: 30 seconds from tap to saved. Fast enough to log between conversations, on a phone, in the field.

  • Tap mic, speak your note naturally
  • AI transcribes using Web Speech API + Whisper fallback
  • AI extracts: commitment made, issues raised, follow-up needed, relationship depth
  • One-tap confirm or edit each field
  • Full transcript always preserved
"Met with Maria today, she's fired up about the school board race. She committed to bringing five people to the training next week..."

Core Feature

Weekly Organizer Reflection

Not a reporting burden—a practice that makes organizers better. Pre-populate everything possible, leave space only for what requires human reflection.

  • headline: What was your headline this week?
  • risks_taken: What risks did you take?
  • risks_avoided: What risks did you back away from?
  • learning: What are you learning?
  • invest_in: One person to invest in next week
  • energy: 1–5 🔋→⚡
